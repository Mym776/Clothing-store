Clothing Shop

Starting our clothing shop was a leap of faith driven by a love for sustainable vintage fashion. We spent months hand-picking and restoring inventory and personally renovating a neglected storefront into an authentic, community-focused space. Our grand opening marked the realization of our goal: to create a vibrant hub for slow fashion enthusiasts, not just another place to shop.
